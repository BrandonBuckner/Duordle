What is Duordle?

Duordle is a small web game based off of the famous New York Times Wordle with a small twist! All you have to do is guess the two words within 8 tries and you win!

How do I play?

You must be on a computer or have a keyboard to play. You can play as a guest or create an account with a unique username. Creating an account will allow you to keep track of your stats. You will notice in this game that the guess boxes are split in half. The top half represent the first answer word and the bottom half represent the other. Light gray means the letter isn't in the word, yellow means it is but in the wrong position, and green means you got the letter and the spot correct!

Each guess word must be exactly five letters in length and must be a valid word. Not every five letter english word is included, but the game will inform you if it isn't. Use the feedback from each guess to maxamize your chance at winning every "round". Good luck Duordling!
